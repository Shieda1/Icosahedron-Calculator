What is the icosahedron?
The icosahedron is a polyhedron that has 20 congruent faces of equilateral triangles. The icosahedron also has 12 vertices and 30 edges.
How to calculate surface area of icosahedron?
To calculate surface area of icosahedron you should use the following formula:
surface area of icosahedron = 5 * (3)^1/2 * (length of any edge)^2
